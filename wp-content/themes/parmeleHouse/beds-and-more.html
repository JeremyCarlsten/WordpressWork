Lewis & Clark Suite

Lewis and Clark Suite - $105/night

Getting up in the morning with east windows is so easy – then again, maybe just 5 more minutes, this bed is sooo comfy. Outfitted to suit you, with a cozy king-size bed, this oversized suite includes an adjoining private bath. Great for vacationers as well as business travelers. Broadband internet is available throughout the home.  Showing a Charles Russell print titled Indians Discovering Lewis and Clark. The prints' actual site is located just south of Plattsmouth. The Lewis & Clark Suite is $105 per evening. This suite also adjoins to the Cow Poke Room.

Mayor's Suite

The Mayor's Suite - $95/Night
We've named this room in honor of former mayor (1962-1964) and owner of the Parmele House, L.W. "Roy" Moore. This is a wonderful airy room with it’s own balcony, bay window, large sitting area, a wonderful queen-size bed and private bath. So comfortable you only come out when the smell of fresh coffee wafts up the stairs! This is our most romantic of them all. Broadband internet is available throughout the home. The Mayor's Suite is $95 per evening.

Cow Poke Room

This room is available only with whole house reservations, such as group events, or for parents with children. It adjoins privately to the Lewis & Clark Suite, offers a full size bed, and is able to keep children within the area while providing a connecting door. Guests staying in the Cow Poke Room share the bathroom with the Lewis and Clark Suite.

 

Price is $25 as an adjoining room for extended family, or $55 as a non-adjoining room. The Cow Poke is only available as a non-adjoining room with a whole-house booking.

Contact us for more information when booking the Cow Poke Room.

Cancellation Policy

Refunds will only be granted if your reservation is cancelled 3 days (72 hours) prior to your reserved date, before 3:00 pm.

Sorry, deposits are not refunded.

If your reservation dates need adjusting, please do not attempt to re-book your room(s) online. Call us and we will try our best to accommodate your new plans. To cancel or adjust your booking, call (402) 296-0525.
